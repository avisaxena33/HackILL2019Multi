  • Players begin the game with 100 health points. Once the health points reach 0, the player is eliminated from the game.
  • Players can accumulate points by answering math problems correctly. If the player answers the problem incorrectly, they will lose points instead.
  • Easy, medium, and hard questions are valued at 3, 5, and 8 points respectively.
  • Stay ahead of the zone by gaining points by solving the problems correctly.
  • Last player standing wins
